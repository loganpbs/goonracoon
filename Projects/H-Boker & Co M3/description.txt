H-Boker & Co M3
---
The M3 by H. Boker and the USMC KA-BAR knife. Did a set of classic military knifes a while back for a PUBG(mobile) trailer animation.
Modelled in Maya, Detailed in Zbrush, textured in Photoshop and Substance painter and rendered in Unreal Engine and Marmoset toolbag4 (Wireframes).
